Project Overview

Zonaly is a Christian-rooted iOS app designed to help users stay focused and walk in purity by limiting access to apps and categories based on their physical location. By integrating geospatial tracking and Apple’s Screen Time API, the app allows users to enforce location-based restrictions on app usage, promoting mindfulness and productivity tailored to specific environments.

Key Features

  • Customizable geofenced locations with adjustable radius.
  • Integration with Apple’s FamilyControls framework to restrict app usage.
  • Intuitive UI built with SwiftUI, including tab-based navigation and modals.
  • Persistent settings using UserDefaults and live region monitoring.
  • App selection using FamilyActivityPicker with real-time count display.

The Impact

Zonaly empowers users—especially teens, families, and accountability partners—to set intentional digital boundaries that align with their values. The app reflects a commitment to purity and self-discipline through technology. With this location-based control, the app empowers individuals to build healthier relationships with technology, fostering productivity and mindfulness tailored to their surroundings.
